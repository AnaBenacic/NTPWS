About

Who is behind this blog?

My name is Ana Benačić and I am greatly interested in all kinds of art.

I was born on January 1st, 1970, in Zagreb, Croatia. From a young age, I started dabbling in the arts. In kindergarden, I started drawing like a little kid does. When I was only 6 years old, I learned how to read. That was a shocker to my mom!

In elementary school things were going steady. I visited the library every Saturday, where I did a lot of different art activities. Clay, collage, painting, you name it!

By the end of elementary school I wrote some theatre plays and played in them. Around that time I also joined an actual theatre group in my town's theatre, but I wasn't in it for long.

I also started writing in my free time around the end of elementary school. Oh, how I miss those times, because my brain was off and I could just write about anything and everything, in pure kid form.

In high school I mostly drew in my school notebooks, as the free time I had was now filled with school work. Who thought 16 subjects at once was a good idea?

Ever since I started college, I slowly picked up on a few of different art hobbies, such as cross stitching, crocheting and diamond painting.

You are now pretty much up to date with who I am and what I like. If you have any questions, please head to the contact page!
